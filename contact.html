Frequently Asked Questions

How long does a typical repair take?

Most repairs are completed within 2-4 hours. Screen replacements and battery changes typically take 1-2 hours. Complex repairs like water damage may take 24-48 hours. We offer same-day service for most common repairs.

Do you offer warranty on repairs?

Yes! All our repairs come with a 6-month warranty. If the same issue occurs within 6 months, we'll fix it for free. The warranty covers both parts and labor.

Can you come to my location for repairs?

Yes, we offer pickup and delivery service within Lagos for an additional fee. For corporate clients, we provide on-site repair services. Contact us for a quote based on your location.

Do you sell original phone parts?

We use high-quality, grade-A replacement parts for all repairs. While we may not always have original manufacturer parts, we guarantee the quality and performance of all parts we install. We offer a range of options to suit different budgets.
