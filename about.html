About Veetech

Your trusted tech partner in Nigeria since 2018

Our Story

Founded in 2018, Veetech started as a small repair shop in Lagos with a passion for technology and a commitment to solving people's tech problems. What began as a one-person operation has grown into a trusted brand serving thousands of customers across Nigeria.

We recognized the growing need for reliable tech solutions in Nigeria - where access to quality repairs and affordable devices was often a challenge. Our mission was clear: provide professional, affordable, and trustworthy tech services to everyone.

Today, we've expanded to offer comprehensive tech solutions including device repairs, sales of smartphones and laptops, power banks, and expert tech consultations. Our team of certified technicians brings years of experience and training to every job.
